DopaBeans Café Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01.09.2025

DopaBeans Café (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and WhatsApp ordering service.

Information We Collect:

  • Your phone number and WhatsApp messages you send us.
  • Order details such as products, sizes, milk types, and preferences.
  • Payment information securely processed via our payment provider.

How We Use Your Information:

  • To process and fulfill your orders accurately.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To manage payments and ensure secure transactions.
  • To send promotional messages only if you have explicitly opted in.

Data Sharing and Security:

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We share your data only with trusted payment processors and service providers essential for operating our ordering system.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or loss.

Your Rights:

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may opt out of promotional communications at any time.
